Story

Conway’s game of life, who hasn't heard of it :) Well for me it has been a toy and a joy for many years. Check Wikipedia for an explanation of Conway’s life. As the default form of it has a tendency to die out, I figured to introduce another life form that interacts with the cellular automata, to keep things going.

Experiment

Uncheck ‘Trails’ to see the raw Conway's life.

Switch on the ‘life rules’ and play with them.

Life controls

Life can be controlled with the Start, Stop, Step and Step back buttons, which are quite self-explanatory.
The following shortcut keys can be used as well:

  • down - stop
  • up - start
  • right - step forward
  • insert - toggle ‘trails’
  • delete - clear life
  • click - draw life
